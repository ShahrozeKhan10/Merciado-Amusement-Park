About Us

Merciado Amusement park is now prime focus of the city and nation. It is made by Seth groups of companies. The development of this park took 5 years to get their desired finished product. It has became nation pride now. The area of theme park is 45 acre land. The Management has put all the efforts to keep the attractions operational.

In Karachi, the credit goes to M/s A.A. Joyland (Pvt) Ltd to build and operate successfully Merciado Amusement Park at Main Rashid Minhas Road. Merciado Amusement Park is Karachi’s most up-to-date leisure recreational area. It’s a place where families can enjoy healthy activities and good fun away from their busy ‘metropolis’ lifestyle.

The amazing family rides in the amusement park are sure to have visitors coming to the park every time they need a little excitement in their routine lifestyle . The objective of the park is to provide quality entertainment to all the visitors regardless of their age. Merciado Park’s remarkable features have been attracting visitors from all walks of life and all age groups ever since its inception a decade ago.

The Merciado Amusement Park is one of its kinds in Karachi. Its quality food outlets offer a delightful choice of a variety of foodstuff and this well kept park, with its 45 acres of land has generous space for having quality time with one’s family and the artificial lake in the park plays an important role, along with adding pleasure to one’s visit to Merciado Amusement Park.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Do you have birthday party packages?
  • A: Yes we do! Please go to the birthday button, or click here.
  • Q: Where can I buy tickets ahead of time?
  • A: You can purchase tickets online right now. Click the “Buy Now Button” above or click here. You can also purchase them at Merciado Administrative Office.
  • Q: Do you take credit cards?
  • A: Yes, we accept American Express, Visa, MasterCard, Discover, Debit Cards and Merciado’s Fun Cards.
  • Q: Do you sell gift cards/certificates?
  • A: No, but we do sell Merciado’s FUNcards! Purchase them at the administrative office, at any ticket window during park hours or by going to our online store.
  • Q: Do you accept checks?
  • A: We do not accept personal checks.
  • Q: Are customers allowed to bring in coolers or outside food?
  • A: Sorry, but no outside food or drinks are allowed into the park.

Park Policies

  • Smoking:
  • Smoking in children’s areas, seating areas, and ride lines is prohibited.
  • Weather:
  • Because Albuquerque has such wonderful weather, we rarely have to close for weather conditions. Safety is our top priority and we will close the rides in case of severe weather; rides will re-open as soon as the storm threat passes. Merciado”s offers a re-admission (“raincheck”) ticket if the weather causes all of the following rides to close for 30 consecutive minutes: New Mexico Rattler, Rocky Mountain Munchies, Cliff Hanger, The Big Flush Water Coaster.
  • Security:
  • Merciado’s Amusement Park is private property and we do not allow firearms or weapons in the Park. We reserve the right to inspect all persons and belongings on Merciado’s property.
  • Service Animals:
  • Your service animal is welcome in Merciado’s Amusement Park. Animals must be in the owner’s control at all times and must be on a harness or leash no longer than four feet. Owners are responsible for the cleaning and disposing of animal waste.
